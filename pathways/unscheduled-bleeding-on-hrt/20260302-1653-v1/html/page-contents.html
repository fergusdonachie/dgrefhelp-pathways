Key management principle: In low-risk women with early unscheduled bleeding on HRT, optimise HRT/progestogen first for up to 6 months; escalate promptly to urgent ultrasound and specialist pathways when timing, severity, persistence, or risk profile indicates higher endometrial risk.

  • Use regimen-specific thresholds and timing (ccHRT vs sHRT).
  • Investigate earlier for heavy/prolonged bleeding or additional cancer-risk factors.
  • Do not delay USOC referral when malignancy concern is present.
