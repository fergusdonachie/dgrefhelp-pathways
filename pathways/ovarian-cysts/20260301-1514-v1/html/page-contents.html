Ovarian cysts: primary care assessment, management and referral

Most ovarian cysts are benign. The pathway should identify emergencies and possible malignancy early while supporting conservative management of low-risk simple cysts.

  • Immediate priorities: exclude torsion/rupture/sepsis and identify suspected cancer features.
  • Primary care work-up: CA125 for suggestive symptom pattern, then pelvic/abdominal ultrasound if CA125 is raised.
  • Referral: urgent cancer-pathway referral for suspicious examination or imaging findings; routine gynaecology referral for persistent symptomatic or uncertain cysts.
