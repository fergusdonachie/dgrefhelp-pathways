## Points requiring clinical review

1. **ABPI >1.4 vs >1.3** — local document uses >1.4 as unreliable threshold; NICE NG237 and SIGN 89 use >1.3. Confirm with vascular team which to use.
2. **Naftidrofuryl stopping rule** — local document implies stop if ineffective after 3 months (unclear whether at 100mg or 200mg). Clarified in this version as: stop if no benefit at either dose after 3 months trial.
3. **Supervised exercise** — local document says "encourage walking"; NICE NG237 specifies supervised exercise programme 2hrs/week. Both included. Confirm local availability.
4. **Hairmyres as named referral centre** — confirm this remains correct for D&G acute vascular emergencies.
