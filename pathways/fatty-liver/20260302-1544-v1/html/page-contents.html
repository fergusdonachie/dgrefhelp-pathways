Key management principle: Most adults with MASLD can be managed in primary care with active cardiometabolic risk reduction and structured fibrosis-risk triage. Refer when fibrosis risk is above threshold or when red-flag liver features are present.

  • Use FIB-4 and LiverRisk for risk stratification.
  • Referral thresholds: FIB-4 >1.3 (<65 years), FIB-4 >2.0 (≥65 years), or LiverRisk >10.
  • Include medication optimisation (including statins where indicated) and weight/metabolic management.
