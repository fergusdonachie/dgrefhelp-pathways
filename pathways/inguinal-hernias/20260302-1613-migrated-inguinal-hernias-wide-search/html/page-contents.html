Key management principle: Triaging inguinal hernia is based on urgency and reducibility. Admit same day for suspected strangulation/obstruction, refer urgently for higher-risk non-emergency cases, and use watchful waiting for selected low-risk reducible cases.

  • Do not delay emergency referral when obstruction/strangulation features are present.
  • All suspected groin hernias in women should be referred urgently.
  • Watchful waiting in selected men requires explicit safety-netting.
