Musculoskeletal Masqueraders

A pathway to help identify non-mechanical causes of widespread pain, fatigue and cognitive symptoms — focusing on Axial Spondyloarthropathy (AxSpA), Spondyloarthropathy (SpA), Connective Tissue Disease (CTD) and Lyme disease.

Consider a non-mechanical cause when patients present with any combination of:

  • Generalised muscle or joint pain
  • Debilitating fatigue
  • Reduced cognition / concentration / "brain fog"
